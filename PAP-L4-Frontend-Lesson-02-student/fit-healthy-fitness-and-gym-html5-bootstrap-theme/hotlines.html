Important Phone Numbers and Resources

Emergency Services (Police, Fire, Medical)

In any emergency situation, dial 911 for immediate assistance.

Phone Number: 911

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

If you or someone you know is struggling with thoughts of suicide, this hotline provides free and confidential support 24/7.

Phone Number: 1-800-273-8255

National Domestic Violence Hotline

If you or someone you know is experiencing domestic violence, this hotline provides support and resources.

Phone Number: 1-800-799-7233

National Helpline for Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services

This helpline provides information, support, and treatment referrals for individuals and families facing substance abuse and mental health issues.

Phone Number: 1-800-662-HELP (1-800-662-4357)

National Low-Income Housing Coalition

This organization advocates for affordable housing policies and provides resources for those seeking affordable housing options.

Phone Number: 202-662-1530

Legal Aid Society

If you need legal assistance but cannot afford an attorney, contact your local Legal Aid Society for support.

Phone Number: (206) 624-2536

National Council on Aging (NCOA)

NCOA provides resources and support for older adults to improve their health, economic security, and well-being.

Phone Number: 571-527-3900

Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline

If you suspect child abuse or are a child in crisis, you can call this hotline for help and support.

Phone Number: 1-800-4-A-CHILD (1-800-422-4453)

Trevor Project Lifeline (LGBTQ+ Youth)

Provides crisis intervention and suicide prevention services for LGBTQ+ youth.

Phone Number: 1-866-488-7386

Veterans Crisis Line

Veterans and their loved ones can call this hotline for confidential support and access to resources in times of crisis.

Phone Number: 1-800-273-8255, Press 1

National Center on Elder Abuse

If you suspect elder abuse or need information on protecting older adults, contact this organization for guidance.

Phone Number: 1-855-500-3537

HIV/AIDS Hotline

This hotline provides information, support, and referrals for individuals and families affected by HIV/AIDS.

Phone Number: 1-800-342-2437

Veterans Crisis Line (Text)

Veterans can also get support via text message by sending a message to 838255. Confidential assistance is available 24/7.

Text Number: 838255

American Red Cross

In times of disaster, the American Red Cross provides relief, shelter, and support. Contact them for assistance and information.

Phone Number: 1-800-733-2767

Pregnancy Resource Center

If you are facing a crisis pregnancy and need support, this center provides counseling, resources, and assistance for expecting mothers.

Phone Number: 1-800-395-HELP (1-800-395-4357)
